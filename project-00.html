Project Context

Bringing the brand online

A manufacturing and distribution company sought to strengthen its online presence as part of an integrated digital marketing strategy. As the UI Designer, I created the user interface and mockups for a website to introduce the company and showcase its legally distributed surface products in the Philippines.

The design emphasized clarity, brand consistency, and a structured presentation of product information to appeal to potential partners and clients.

** Some text content and visual elements, including logos, website name, images and color palettes, have been modified to comply with NDA restrictions.

Goal

Aiming for a stronger
presence online

The visual interface aimed to establish a stronger online presence beyond the client’s existing Facebook outlet. By creating a simple, elegant, and professional design, the goal is to attract more organic visitors and enhance the company’s digital credibility.

Visual Strategy

On Brand, By Design

The visual design closely followed the company’s existing brand identity, including colors, tone, and structure. But due to NDA restrictions, the presented version uses a modified branding system that maintains the original’s professional tone. Fonts and logos were replaced with alternatives (e.g., Logoipsum) while preserving the overall look and feel. The goal was to ensure clarity, credibility, and consistency throughout the interface.
